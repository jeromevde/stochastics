The Vasicek model SDE is dr = $\kappa(\theta$ − r)dt + $\sigma$ dB. The term $\kappa(\theta$ − r) represents:
$\kappa(\theta$ − r) is the mean-reversion drift: when r > $\theta$ the drift is negative (pulling r down), and when r < $\theta$ it's positive (pulling r up). $\kappa$ = speed, $\theta$ = level.
