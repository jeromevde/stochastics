The Heston model has how many parameters to calibrate (for a risk-neutral model)?
Five parameters: $\kappa$ (mean-reversion speed), $\theta$ (long-run variance), $\xi$ (vol-of-vol), $\rho$ (correlation), and $\sigma^2$(0) (initial variance). The drift $\mu$ = r is fixed by risk-neutrality.
