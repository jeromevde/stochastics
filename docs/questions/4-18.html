If dx = −$\kappa$x dt + $\sigma$ dB and f(x,t) = xe^{$\kappa$t}, then by Itô's lemma df equals:
df = $f_t$ dt + $f_x$ dx = $\kappa$xe^{$\kappa$t} dt + e^{$\kappa$t}(−$\kappa$x dt + $\sigma$ dB) = $\sigmae^{\kappa$t} dB. The drift terms cancel, leaving a pure Brownian integral — key to solving the Vasicek SDE.
